Presenting Greenhouse


Greenhouse the app is designed to help you optimize your household and lifestyle to:

  • Save energy
  • Save money
  • Reduce your carbon footprint!

Using it is simple: enter your metrics, ex. energy use costs, watt usage, and Greenhouse will suggest a possible renewable energy solution that will reduce utility cost and energy use, providing a result that is better for the customer and for the environment.

Examples of solutions can be the installation of solar panels, suggestions of different appliances that are more energy efficient, or switching to LED lights.
